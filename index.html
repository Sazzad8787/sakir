My Vision & Goals

My vision is to transform our village into a model community where every resident has access to basic amenities, quality education, healthcare, and employment opportunities. I aim to create transparent governance, encourage community participation in decision-making, and implement sustainable development projects that benefit all sections of society.

Key Objectives:

  • 100% road connectivity to all households
  • Clean drinking water supply to every family
  • Digital literacy programs for youth
  • Women empowerment through skill development
  • Environment conservation initiatives
  • Transparent fund utilization and reporting
